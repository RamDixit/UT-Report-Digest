Smartphone Voice Commands

Siri, Google Now, and Cortana

UserTesting
Ram Dixit

Study Overview

While looking up the weather, participants were unable to ask multiple questions in single command. Having to split their question up disrupted the fluidity of the interaction.


Finding a restaurant near a landmark proved to be difficult, as commands like “Restaurants near Carmike 16 movie theater” were misunderstood as searches for the movie theater itself.


While quite convenient, these systems still struggled with context, providing accurate but sometimes irrelevant results to participants.
